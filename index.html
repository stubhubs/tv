How do I track my Temu order?

You can track your order by logging into your Temu account and visiting the "Orders" section. For immediate assistance, call our customer service at 1-805-635-0141.

What is Temu's return policy?

Temu offers a 90-day return window for most items. Contact our support team at 1-805-635-0141 to initiate a return and get a prepaid shipping label.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 6-20 business days. Express shipping options are available. Call 1-805-635-0141 for shipping questions.

How do I contact Temu customer service?

You can reach us 24/7 by calling 1-805-635-0141, emailing help@temu.com, or using the live chat in the Temu app.
